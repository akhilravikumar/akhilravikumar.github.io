Novel self-assembled dendritic nanomaterials for biomedical applications

To develop novel self-assembled dendritic structures that can be used for various biomedical applications such as drug delivery, tissue engineering, and bioimaging. The project will apply fundamentals from chemical sciences to prepare these self-assembled structures and then test these materials for their biological properties.
